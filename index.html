About Us

Welcome to Rishi Ganga Group

Rishi Ganga Group is a trusted service provider based in Uttarakhand, dedicated to delivering reliable travel and business solutions. Our flagship division, Kunjapuri Tours and Travels, specializes in providing comfortable and memorable travel experiences across Uttarakhand and nearby destinations.

We offer a wide range of services including Char Dham Yatra tours, Uttarakhand tour packages, local and outstation taxi services, airport transfers, hotel booking assistance, and customized family and group travel arrangements. Along with travel services, the group also operates Multi Business Services and Micro Finance Assistance to support individuals and small businesses with reliable solutions.
